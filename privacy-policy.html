Privacy Policy

Last updated — May 27, 2026

Aqualog does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All information you enter (water logs, preferences, profile) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No Analytics & No Tracking

Aqualog contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking code of any kind. The app does not use cookies, device identifiers, or internet access.

Core Motion

If you grant accelerometer permission, Aqualog uses Core Motion solely to animate the water surface based on device tilt. This data is processed in real time on-device and is never stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Aqualog does not integrate any third-party services, frameworks, or APIs that collect data.

Your Control

You can delete all app data at any time via Settings → Reset All Data or by uninstalling the app.
